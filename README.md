# BMW-Sales-Performance-Analysis
Sales analysis across 2015-2024
## Objective
Analyze multi-year BMW sales data to evaluate revenue trends, identify key growth drivers, and assess market-level volatility across countries and product models.

## Dataset
The dataset includes:
- Sales revenue
- Units sold
- Country
- Vehicle model
- Transaction date (2015–2024)

## Key Analysis Performed
- Year-over-Year (YoY) revenue growth
- Quarter-over-Quarter (QoQ) performance comparison using DAX
- Country-level revenue contribution analysis
- Model-level growth driver identification
- Volatility assessment across markets

## Key Insights
- Total revenue across 2015–2024 reached approximately USD 48B, with 633K units sold.
- Overall YoY growth averaged 12.04%, though performance showed significant volatility before stabilizing post-2020.
- Growth in 2024 was primarily driven by Brazil, Canada, UK, and India.
- Revenue increases were concentrated in specific models (5 Series, 7 Series, i7, X3, X5), indicating selective product-driven growth rather than uniform performance across all models.

## Business Implications
- Revenue growth is dependent on select high-performing models.
- Market volatility indicates the need for localized strategy rather than global assumptions.
- Further investigation into underperforming markets may support performance optimization.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX (Time Intelligence Functions)
- Data Modeling
- SQL (Data Preparation/ Querying)
